Programs

We offer programs for children ranging from infant to school age. If you have any questions about these programs, please feel free to contact us.

Infants

Our infant area provides the highest quality care in an environment that is designed with the parent and child in mind. While in our care, the children are stimulated through music, exercise, outdoor nature walk time, facial and verbal mimicking as well as lots of hugs and tickles.

Toddlers

At this age, children are meeting new challenges as fast as they come. Crawling, walking and eventually running are levels they accomplish in what seems like a blink of an eye. Children learn best if they are in a stimulating environment. Music, stories, outdoor play, and learning simplicities envelop much of the day. Our caregivers are dedicated in helping each child develop these skills through a trusting and loving relationship. A parental daily report is given to update each family on meal times, nap times, diaper change and all activities accomplished each day.

Preschool

At this stage, children will experience huge thinking, learning, social, and emotional changes that will help them to explore their new world, and make sense of it. During this stage, toddlers soon to be preschoolers are also learning how able to follow two- or three-step directions, sort objects by shape and color, imitate the actions of adults and playmates, and express a wide range of emotions. Our planned and scaffolded daily learning activities provides balance to help them to begin and engage in activities meaningfully. At this stage the teachers are working on the children to help them self-regulate, potty training is one of them.

A parental daily report is given to update each family on meal times, nap times, toilet training and all activities accomplished each day this encourage families to be a part of the foundational learning of their children
